Privacy Policy

Moonspill by Tinydeskco Inc.
Effective: March 23, 2026

Overview

Moonspill is a divination app created by Tinydeskco Inc. We believe your spiritual practice is personal. This policy explains what data Moonspill collects (very little) and how it's handled.

The short version: your data stays on your device. We don't track you, we don't sell your data, and we don't run ads.

Data Stored on Your Device

Moonspill stores the following information locally on your device using standard on-device storage. This data never leaves your phone unless you explicitly choose to share it (e.g., sharing a sigil image or a reading with a friend).

  • Display name and zodiac sign (entered during onboarding)
  • Saved readings and card pull history (Grimoire)
  • Created sigils and intentions
  • App preferences (daily reminder setting, onboarding status)
  • Cached subscription tier for faster loading

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed.

Account Creation & Social Features

Moonspill may offer optional account creation and social features in the future, such as adding friends and sharing readings. If introduced:

  • Account creation will be optional — the app will remain fully functional without an account.
  • We will collect only the minimum information necessary (e.g., display name, email for authentication).
  • Shared readings and content will only be visible to users you explicitly choose to share with.
  • You will be able to delete your account and all associated data at any time.

This policy will be updated with specific details before any social features are launched.

Subscription & Payment Data

Moonspill offers optional paid subscriptions (Full Moon and Blood Moon tiers) processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status.

  • We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID.
  • RevenueCat receives an anonymous identifier to track your subscription status. No personal information is shared with RevenueCat.
  • Subscription management (cancellation, renewal, billing) is handled through your Apple ID settings.

For RevenueCat's privacy practices, see: revenuecat.com/privacy

Data We Do Not Collect

To be clear, Moonspill does not:

  • Collect personal information beyond what you voluntarily enter in the app
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Display advertisements
  • Share or sell any user data to third parties
  • Require account creation or login
  • Access your contacts, camera, microphone, or location
  • Store any data on external servers

Sigil Sharing

When you choose to download or share a sigil, the image is saved to your device or shared using your phone's native share functionality. Moonspill does not upload sigil images to any server.

Children's Privacy

Moonspill is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us and we will delete it.
